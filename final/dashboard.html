Should you go for 2 to go up by 9?

NFL analysis using the nflfastR XGBoost WP model called directly, plus team EPA analysis from real games.

Two layers of analysis

WP Calculator: Calls the nflfastR WP model with exact inputs — score margin, time, home/away, timeouts. For an average team, what's the EV of going for 2? Always clean, always monotonic, no averaging artifacts.
EPA Analysis: Filters actual games by team quality to see if the story changes when elite offenses or bad defenses are involved.
